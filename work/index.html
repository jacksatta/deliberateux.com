Technology

Enterprise Orchestration

Problem

Agents navigating 12+ disconnected systems during customer interactions. High training overhead, inconsistent service quality, and significant operational friction.

Each system had its own UI patterns, data models, and workflows—forcing agents to mentally translate between contexts while customers waited.

Solution

Designed a unified orchestration layer that surfaced contextual information and actions based on customer journey state. Progressive disclosure reduced cognitive load while maintaining access to full system capabilities.

Created a component library enabling consistent interaction patterns across all integrated systems, with real-time data synchronization.

Outcome

40% reduction in average handle time. Training time for new agents decreased from 6 weeks to 3 weeks. Customer satisfaction scores improved 18%.

Finserv

Omnichannel Platform

Problem

Customer service representatives operating on legacy systems disconnected from digital channels, customer profiles, and service history. Agents couldn't provide seamless service across touchpoints.

Solution

Designed an omnichannel platform unifying customer data, case history, and transaction records. Agent-first interface with real-time synchronization across all service channels.

Built guided workflows for complex financial transactions that reduced errors and compliance issues.

Outcome

Unified customer view across all service channels. Transaction errors reduced 65%. Agent onboarding time cut in half.

Retail

Email Analytics Platform

Problem

Global marketing teams lacked visibility into email campaign performance across regions. No centralized way to browse historical campaigns, compare engagement patterns, or understand what drove clicks.

Solution

Designed a searchable email library with click overlay analytics. Teams could browse campaigns by region, date, and category, then view heat-mapped click data directly on rendered emails.

Built filtering and comparison tools to surface top-performing templates and identify engagement patterns across campaign types.

Outcome

Centralized visibility across global campaigns. Reduced time to find reference emails from hours to seconds. Enabled data-driven template decisions based on actual click behavior.

Telecom

AI-Powered Revenue Intelligence

Challenge

Marketing and sales teams operating in silos with no intelligent handoff. Campaign engagement data wasn't surfacing to sales in actionable ways, making it difficult to prioritize leads and time outreach effectively.

Renewal conversations lacked context—CSMs and AEs had no unified view of account health, engagement signals, or AI-driven recommendations to guide strategic discussions.

Vision

Designed a future-state concept bridging marketing and sales through intelligent engagement scoring and prioritization. Campaign interactions flow directly into account records, with AI surfacing the most promising opportunities based on strategic importance.

Introduced Einstein Coach for real-time AI guidance during renewal conversations—surfacing cross-sell opportunities, handling objection patterns, and recommending next best actions. For low-risk renewals, envisioned Agentforce autonomously generating quotes and capturing revenue through 1-click acceptance.

Impact

This vision work demonstrated how AI could transform the marketing-to-sales handoff and free skilled teams for strategic work. The concepts influenced roadmap discussions around autonomous revenue capture and intelligent account prioritization.

Retail / Telecom

Retail Tech Platform

Problem

Store operations fragmented across multiple systems—inventory management, scheduling, customer service, and reporting tools all operating independently.

Solution

Designed integrated operations platform consolidating daily workflows into unified interface. Role-based dashboards surfaced relevant tasks and metrics for different store positions.

Built offline-first architecture ensuring reliability in variable connectivity environments.

Outcome

Daily operational tasks reduced from 45 minutes to 15 minutes per store. Inventory accuracy improved 28%. Manager satisfaction scores increased significantly.
